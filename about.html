About Me

I am Leki Wangmo, third year B.Ed student majoring in Information Technology. I hail from a remote village of Tshogoenpa under Trashigang Dzongkhag, which gets its name from the legend of nine brothers of the lake. Despite my love for travel and having a family of five including my mother, I unfortunately lost my mother during ninth grade. Exploring new places, immersing myself in diverse cultures, and experiencing the beauty of different landscapes truly fuels my spirit. Beyond that, communicating with others is another part of my life that brings me immense joy and fulfillment.
